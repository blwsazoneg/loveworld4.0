Frequently Asked Questions

You can view your complete order history and the status of each order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" page, which is accessible from the user dropdown menu in the top-right corner. The status will update from "Paid" to "Shipped" once your order is on its way.

We process all payments securely through Stripe. You can use any major credit or debit card, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. All transactions are encrypted and handled on Stripe's secure payment page.

To become a seller, you must first create a user account. Once you are logged in, navigate to your "My Profile" page. There you will find a "Become a Seller" form. Fill out your company details and submit the application. Our admin team will review it, and upon approval, your account will be upgraded to an SBO, allowing you to list products.
